Easy Booking & Scheduled Rides

UniRide offers a fast, simple, and reliable booking experience for students. No stress. No last-minute rushing. Just tap, book, and ride.

✔ How It Works

  • Open the UniRide app
  • Select pickup & drop-off location
  • Choose a time or use "Next Available Ride"
  • Confirm booking instantly

✔ Benefits of Scheduled Rides

  • Saves time & reduces waiting
  • No price surge fixed student-friendly cost
  • Perfect for early classes & late evening rides

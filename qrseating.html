Organized Seating with QR Code

UniRide uses a smart QR system to eliminate rushing, crowding, and confusion. Every student gets a unique seat QR code that confirms their spot.

✔ Features

  • Unique QR for each student
  • Scan to confirm seat & enter the bus
  • No overcrowding or seat fights
  • Makes check-ins 3× faster

✔ Safety & Tracking

  • Verified student identity
  • Real-time bus GPS tracking
  • Parents can monitor routes
  • Increased campus transportation safety<

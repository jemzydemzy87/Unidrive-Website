About UniRide

UniRide is a student-first campus transportation service focused on mobility, affordability, and eco-friendly solutions.

Our Mission

To make campus transportation seamless, safe, and sustainable through student-powered mobility systems.

Why UniRide?

  • Verified student drivers
  • Eco-conscious green movement
  • Smart matching for ride sharing
